Sleep Health and Lifestyle Dataset Codebook

Variable / Data Type / Measurement Unit / Description 
------------------------------------------------------

Person ID / identifier (integer) / -- / Unique identifier for each participant
Gender / categorical (nominal) / -- / The gender of the person (Male/Female)
Age / numerical (continuous) / years / Age of the participant 
Sleep Duration / numerical (continuous) / hours / The number of hours the person sleeps per day
Quality of Sleep / numerical (discrete/integer) / 1-10 scale / A subjective rating of the quality of sleep, ranging from 1 to 10
Physical Activity Level / numerical (continuous) / minutes / The number of minutes the person engages in physical activity each day
Stress Level / numerical (discrete/integer) / 1-10 scale / A subjective rating of the stress level experienced by the person, ranging from 1 to 10
Heart Rate / numerical (continuous) / beats per minute (bpm) / The resting heart rate of the person in beats per minute
Daily Steps / numerical (discrete) / steps / The number of steps the person takes each day 
Blood Pressure / numerical (continuous) / mmHg / The blood pressure measurement of the person, indicated as systolic pressure over diastolic pressure
Systolic / numerical (continuous) / mmHg / Systolic blood pressure value extracted from the original Blood Pressure value
Diastolic / numerical (continuous) / mmHg / Diastolic blood pressure value extracted from the original blood pressure value
BMI Category / categorical (nominal) / -- / The BMI category of a person (Underweight, Normal, Overweight) 
Sleep Disorder / categorical (nominal) / -- / The presence or absence of a sleep disorder in the person (None, Insomnia, Sleep Apnea)
Occupation / categorical (nominal) / -- / The occupation of a person (Nurse, Doctor, Engineer, Lawyer, Teacher, Accountant, Salesperson, Scientist, Software Engineer, Sales Representative) 
Occupation Group / categorical (nominal) / -- / Occupation grouped into broader categories (Healthcare, Technology, Sales, Education)
